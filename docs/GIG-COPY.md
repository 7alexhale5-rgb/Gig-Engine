## Gig Copy Formula

**Title format:** `I will [specific deliverable] using [tool/platform] for your [business type]`

**Description structure:**
1. Opening hook (pain point, 2 sentences)
2. What you get (tier breakdown)
3. Why me (CTO + AI-powered speed)
4. Process (3 steps)
5. FAQ (5 questions)
6. CTA ("Message me before ordering")
