### FAQ

1. **Q: Should I use GA4 or Mixpanel?**
   A: GA4 is free and solid for most businesses -- especially if you run Google Ads. Mixpanel is better for product analytics (SaaS, apps) where you need to track user behavior over time. I will recommend the right tool based on your business model during our kickoff.

2. **Q: My GA4 is set up but I do not trust the data. Can you fix it?**
   A: Yes. This is actually the most common request I get. Most GA4 setups have duplicate events, missing conversions, or filters that exclude real traffic. I audit your existing setup, fix what is broken, and fill in what is missing.

3. **Q: Will tracking slow down my website?**
   A: No. I implement tracking through Google Tag Manager with proper trigger timing so scripts load after your page content. Premium includes server-side tracking which has zero impact on page load speed.

4. **Q: Can you track events inside my web app (not just page views)?**
   A: Yes. Button clicks, form submissions, scroll depth, video engagement, file downloads, in-app actions -- anything a user does can be tracked as a custom event. This is where analytics gets useful. Page views alone tell you almost nothing.

5. **Q: What about privacy and GDPR compliance?**
   A: I implement tracking with consent management in mind. If you need a cookie consent banner, I can configure one. Server-side tracking (Premium) gives you first-party data collection that respects privacy regulations while still giving you the data you need.
