## Service Description

Most businesses are paying for 8-12 software tools that don't talk to each other, running manual processes that should have been automated years ago, and lacking visibility into which systems actually earn their monthly cost. A structured automation audit identifies exactly where you're leaking time and money — and creates a clear path to fix it.

I conduct comprehensive audits that cover your complete tool ecosystem, data flows between systems, manual processes ripe for automation, redundant tools that can be consolidated, and security gaps in your integrations. Every audit produces a prioritized roadmap ranked by ROI — so you know what to automate first, what to consolidate, and what to leave alone.

This isn't theoretical consulting. I'm a practicing CTO who builds and maintains enterprise automation systems. Every recommendation I make is something I can also implement, which means the roadmap is grounded in real technical feasibility, not wishful thinking.
